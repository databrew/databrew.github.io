R/Data Training for Businesses & Organizations

According to the IDC, 53% of large Canadian organizations identified the lack of talent as the biggest impediment to successful completion of big data projects.

What we offer

databrew offers custom-designed R and data training to equip your employees with the data science skills they need to better contribute to your organization’s success.

Our team works with you to understand your company and objectives to design a training program that best supports your unique needs. Choose from on-site group classes, one-on-one sessions, lunch-and-learns, weekend data bootcamps, or a combination. Our workshop format and content can be easily adapted to suit your organization and your learners.

    Introduction to R for Data Analysis

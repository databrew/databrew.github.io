    Joe Brew

    Joe is a data scientist, epidemiologist, and economist. He has worked with the Florida Department of Health (USA), the Chicago Department of Public Health (USA), the Barcelona Public Health Agency (Spain), and the Tigray Regional Health Bureau (Ethiopia). His research focuses on the economics of malaria and its elimination.

    Joe earned his BA at Sewanee: The University of the South (2008), his MA at the Institut Catholique de Paris (2009) and his MPH at the Kobenhavns Universitet (2013).

    Joe is passionate about international development, infectious disease surveillance, and data. He also likes cycling, running and pizza.

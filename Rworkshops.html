FAQ

Who can sign up for a workshop?
Academics, professionals, and any other individuals interested in learning R.

Where do you offer the workshops?
Currently, we offer workshops in downtown Toronto (Canada), Manhica and Maputo (Mozambique), and Barcelona (Spain).

Do you offer discounts?
In addition to student discounts, we want to ensure that our classes are accessbile to individuals of all socioeconomic situations, and thus offer discounts to those who cannot afford the regular tuition. For this purposes, we have a limited scholarship fund. Please get in touch if this applies to you.

Data Science Consulting & Services

What we offer

We offer customized solutions and approaches for your organization's analytical needs. Our services range from the short and sweet (one-off analyses) to the self-reproducing (dashboard construction and app development) to the big and bold (collaborative software development with integrated team capacity-building).

How we're different

We don't work for clients, we work with them. Our solutions are generally aimed not at solving a specific problem, but rather equipping an organization with the toolset to solve the problem for themselves. This means (a) empowered employees and (b) the ability to re-apply solutions to other areas.

Examples

We've previously worked with organizations on projects like:

  • Implementing a daily emergency room symptomatic surveillance anomaly detection report
  • Building a custom R package for a research institution
  • Optimizing school system food acquisition and delivery networks

Introduction to R for Data Analysis

In this course, participants learn to navigate the RStudio environment and read in different datasets. They also learn the fundamentals of cleaning, analyzing, and visualizing real data. There is time immediately following the course for participants to ask questions and get support from the instructor with their own data, if interested.
